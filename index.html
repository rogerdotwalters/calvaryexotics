Who We Are

Passionate about exotics. Committed to quality.

Calvary Exotics was founded by hobbyists who grew into dedicated breeders with one goal: produce healthy, well-socialized exotic animals that thrive in their new homes. Every animal in our collection is raised with meticulous care, proper husbandry, and genuine love for the craft.

We specialize in crested geckos, viper geckos, ball pythons, bioactive isopods, and a rotating selection of rare species. Whether you're a first-time keeper or a seasoned collector, we take the time to match you with the right animal and give you the knowledge to succeed.
